Holfuy Upgrader is an Android application for updating the firmware of compatible Holfuy weather stations over a USB connection.

The application communicates directly with the station's built-in Nuvoton ISP (In-System Programming) bootloader, allowing firmware supplied by Holfuy to be installed from an Android phone or tablet.

Features

• Firmware updates over USB
• Simple, straightforward user interface
• Automatic detection of supported Holfuy weather stations
• Compatible with Android 8.0 (API 26) and later
• Open-source software

Requirements

• Android device with USB OTG support
• USB OTG adapter (if required by your device)
• USB cable with a USB Micro-B connector for the weather station
• Firmware image supplied by Holfuy

Holfuy Upgrader does not collect personal information, display advertising, or require a user account. All communication occurs locally between your Android device and the connected weather station.

Source code, documentation, and the Privacy Policy are available at:
