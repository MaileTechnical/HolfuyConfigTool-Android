# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** July 2026

Holfuy Upgrader is designed to update the firmware of Holfuy weather stations over a direct USB connection.

## Information Collection

Holfuy Upgrader does **not** collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information.

The application does not:

- require user accounts or sign-in
- collect personal information
- collect location information
- collect device identifiers
- collect analytics or usage statistics
- display advertising
- use advertising identifiers
- transmit data to the developer, Holfuy, or any third party

## USB Communication

The application communicates directly with compatible Holfuy weather stations using a USB connection to perform firmware updates.

All communication occurs locally between the Android device and the connected weather station.
Firmware files selected by the user are transferred directly to the weather station and are not transmitted over the Internet.

## Third-Party Services

Holfuy Upgrader does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

The application is distributed through Google Play. Google may collect information as described
in Google's own privacy policies and terms of service. This Privacy Policy applies only to Holfuy Upgrader.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

If this Privacy Policy changes, the updated version will be published in this repository and, where applicable, made available through Google Play.
